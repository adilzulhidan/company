About Us

ZulhidanCorp is a leading company focused on developing and implementing Internet of Things (IoT) solutions for various industrial sectors. Founded in 2020, we are committed to delivering advanced technologies that connect devices, systems, and people to create a more efficient, intelligent, and sustainable ecosystem.

Vision To be a leader in IoT innovation that connects the world in a smarter, more efficient, and more sustainable way.

Mission Technology Innovation: Developing cutting-edge IoT solutions to meet customer needs and support industry growth. Improving Quality of Life: Creating technology that makes everyday life easier and increases productivity. Sustainability: Implementing solutions that support environmental sustainability through energy savings and waste reduction. Education and Awareness: Increasing public knowledge about IoT through training programs and seminars. Strategic Partnerships: Building partnerships with other companies and government agencies to expand the adoption of IoT technology.
